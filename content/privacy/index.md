## Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 3, 2025

Tech Content Curator ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect and use information when you visit our site.

### Information We Collect

We use **Google Analytics 4** to understand how visitors use our site. This service collects:

- Pages you visit
- How long you stay on each page
- Your approximate location (city/country level)
- Device type (mobile/desktop) and browser
- How you found our site (search, social media, direct link)

Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to collect this information.

### How We Use This Information

We use analytics data to:
- Understand which content is most popular
- Improve site navigation and user experience
- Identify technical issues
- Plan future content topics

We do **NOT**:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Track you across other websites
- Collect personally identifiable information
- Use data for advertising targeting

### Your Data Rights

**For EU/UK visitors:**
- You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data
- You can object to processing or request data portability
- You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies
- Contact us at: hardcoreprawn@github.com

**For all visitors:**
- You can decline cookies using the banner on your first visit
- You can block cookies in your browser settings
- You can opt out of Google Analytics with the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

### Cookies

This site uses **opt-in cookie consent**. Google Analytics cookies are only set after you accept the cookie banner.

Cookies we use after consent:

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| _ga | Google Analytics - distinguish users | 2 years |
| _ga_* | Google Analytics - persist session state | 2 years |
| cookieconsent_status | Stores your cookie consent choice | 1 year |

### Data Storage and Security

Analytics data is processed by Google and stored on Google's servers. Google Analytics is configured with IP anonymization enabled.
